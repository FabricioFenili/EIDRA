# Research Factory Operating Model

## Mission
Convert governed features into tested hypotheses, candidate signals, registered factors, and institutionally reviewable research output.

## Three canonical research paradigms
1. time-series predictability
2. cross-sectional predictability
3. macro regime predictability

## Flow
`feature store -> experiment design -> experiment execution -> diagnostics -> factor triage -> model candidate -> registry`

## First V48 research target
PTAX USD/BRL momentum predictability under time-series methods.

## Required experiment metadata
- experiment_id
- dataset_version
- feature_set_version
- model_class
- training_window
- evaluation_window
- calibration_method
- diagnostic summary
- decision outcome
