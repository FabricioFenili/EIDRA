# Marketing Operating Model

## Mission
Convert validated system intelligence into institutional narrative, product positioning, distribution, and monetization.

## Responsibilities
- brand architecture
- product communication
- scientific communication governance
- distribution strategy
- revenue packaging
- market education

## Non-negotiable principles
- marketing does not create technical truth
- marketing does not promise profits
- marketing does not outrun research maturity
- all public claims must be auditable by the council

## Workflow
technical scope -> council validation -> marketing translation -> institutional packaging -> distribution
