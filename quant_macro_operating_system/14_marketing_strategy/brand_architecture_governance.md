SECTION 2 — BRAND GOVERNANCE PROTOCOL
================================================================

All brand, narrative and communication activities follow the governance rules below.

Objective:

• Translate science into clarity and adoption
• Maintain alignment with the EIDRA system architecture
• Prevent generative drift or speculative narratives
• Ensure all outputs remain auditable by the Council

---------------------------------------------------------------

CORE COMMUNICATION PRINCIPLE

All communication must follow the hierarchy:

Clarity → Strategy → Science

This ensures the public:

1. Understands the message
2. Recognizes strategic value
3. Trusts scientific authority

Science must always be translated into actionable strategic insight.

---------------------------------------------------------------

ANTI‑HALLUCINATION PROTOCOL

The following rules apply to all communications:

1. No speculative claims about predictive certainty
2. No exaggerated marketing promises
3. All strategic claims must trace back to:
   - models
   - research
   - methodology
4. All narratives must be auditable by the Council

---------------------------------------------------------------

NARRATIVE BOUNDARIES

Communication must avoid:

• sensationalism
• exaggerated futurism
• speculative macro predictions without model support

EIDRA communicates as:

Strategic. Scientific. Precise.

---------------------------------------------------------------

BRAND POSITIONING

EIDRA is positioned as:

An Architecture of Strategic Intelligence.

Purpose:

Transform complex economic and financial data into directional insight
for decision‑making.

Brand perception goals:

• authority
• clarity
• intellectual rigor
• accessibility

---------------------------------------------------------------

APPROVAL WORKFLOW

All branding artifacts follow the process:

Agent Draft
↓
VP Review
↓
Council Audit
↓
Council Approval

Only after Council approval may materials be published.
