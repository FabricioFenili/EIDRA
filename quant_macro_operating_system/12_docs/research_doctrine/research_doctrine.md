# Research Doctrine

## Objective
Define the lifecycle of hypotheses, signals, factors, and models.

## Lifecycle
1. hypothesis intake
2. preliminary plausibility review
3. econometric validation
4. robustness and replication
5. factor registration
6. portfolio integration eligibility
7. performance monitoring
8. decay monitoring
9. retirement or redesign

## Mandatory controls
- walk-forward validation
- cross-regime validation
- multiple testing control
- decision ledger entry
- model retirement criteria
