# VP Canon — Macro Intelligence

## Mandate
Growth, inflation, liquidity, policy path, regime inference, scenarios, fragility.

## Core canon
1. Macroeconomics — Olivier Blanchard
2. Monetary Policy Strategy — Frederic Mishkin
3. Time Series Analysis — James Hamilton
4. Forecasting: Principles and Practice
5. Nowcasting: The Real-Time Informational Content of Macroeconomic Data — Giannone, Reichlin, Small
6. Macroeconomic Nowcasting and Forecasting with Big Data — Bok et al.

## Why these matter
- Blanchard gives the integrated macro framework.
- Mishkin gives policy transmission and monetary regime thinking.
- Hamilton is the core technical foundation for modern time-series macro work.
- Giannone/Reichlin/Small and Bok et al. ground nowcasting and big-data macro monitoring.
