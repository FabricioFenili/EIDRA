# VP Canon — Risk & Resilience

## Mandate
Market/factor risk, tail/ruin risk, liquidity contagion, stress survival.

## Core canon
1. Quantitative Risk Management — McNeil, Frey, Embrechts
2. Financial Risk Forecasting — Jon Danielsson
3. Market Microstructure Theory — Maureen O'Hara

## Supporting canon
1. Asset Pricing
2. Time Series Analysis
3. Macroeconomics

## Why these matter
- QRM is the deepest institutional risk text in the canon.
- Danielsson adds practical market-risk forecasting and implementation discipline.
- O'Hara matters because liquidity, market structure, and information asymmetry are risk objects, not just execution objects.

## Development priorities
- factor/risk decomposition
- ruin probability
- liquidity spiral analysis
- crisis simulation
- early warning governance
