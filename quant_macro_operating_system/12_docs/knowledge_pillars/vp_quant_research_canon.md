# VP Canon — Quant Research

## Mandate
Alpha discovery, econometric validation, backtest robustness, and research governance.

## Core canon
1. The Elements of Statistical Learning
2. The Econometrics of Financial Markets — Campbell, Lo, MacKinlay
3. Advances in Financial Machine Learning — Lopez de Prado
4. Causal Inference for Statistics, Social, and Biomedical Sciences — Imbens, Rubin
5. Statistical Rethinking — McElreath

## Why these matter
- ESL gives the general language of learning and model comparison.
- Campbell/Lo/MacKinlay grounds market data analysis in rigorous econometrics.
- Lopez de Prado addresses multiple testing, labeling, and practical research pitfalls.
- Imbens/Rubin protects the organization from false causal narratives.
- McElreath improves probabilistic judgment and model interpretability.

## Development priorities
- experiment registry
- replication control
- anti-overfitting audit
- walk-forward validation
- multiple-testing control

## Exclusions
- black-box prediction without diagnostics
- retrospective story-fitting
- strategy claims without replication
