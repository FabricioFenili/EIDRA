# VP Canon — Performance & Learning

## Mandate
Performance attribution, diagnostics, post-mortems, experimentation, organizational learning.

## Core canon
1. Trustworthy Online Controlled Experiments — Kohavi, Tang, Xu
2. Causal Inference for Statistics, Social, and Biomedical Sciences — Imbens, Rubin
3. Statistical Rethinking — McElreath
4. The Signal and the Noise — Nate Silver

## Why these matter
- Kohavi/Tang/Xu establishes experimentation discipline.
- Imbens/Rubin prevents false learning from observational noise.
- McElreath improves uncertainty reasoning.
- Nate Silver is useful for executive calibration under noisy predictive settings.

## Development priorities
- measurement discipline
- learning loops
- pre/post-mortem quality
- attribution standards
- decision ledger calibration
